# Module: two_column_details

## When to use
- Side-by-side comparison or two related detail blocks.

## Allowed edits
- Column headers (max 20 characters as indicated in the snippet).
- Body copy (max 7 lines as indicated in the snippet).

## Never change
- Column widths, backgrounds, borders, or typography.
- Padding and spacing.

## Example labels / parameters
- Left header: <LEFT_TITLE>
- Left body: <LEFT_BODY>
- Right header: <RIGHT_TITLE>
- Right body: <RIGHT_BODY>

## Styling constraints
- Keep left header on light grey and right header on purple as provided.
- Keep body text font sizes unchanged.
