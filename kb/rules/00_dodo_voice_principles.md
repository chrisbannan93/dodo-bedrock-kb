# Dodo Voice Principles

## Brand promise: The Real Deal
- **Belief — Real Smart:** Save and spend your money on what you want, not just what you need.
- **Purpose — Real Enjoyment:** Connect Aussies to the things they love (mates, family, streaming, gaming).
- **Foundation — Real Value:** Great products at a great price with solid customer service (value-led, not always the cheapest).

## How customers should feel
- Good value for money
- Genuine
- Trustworthy
- Smart
- Like Dodo is for someone like them

## Brand personality
- Honest
- Bold
- Smart
- Surprising
- Approachable

## Tone
- Friendly
- Witty
- Helpful
- Relatable (but not overly personal)
- Confident
- Purposeful
- Playful

## Language principles
- Human, conversational, relaxed
- Easy to understand and authentic
- Simplify without dumbing down
- Connect benefits back to the customer

## Purpose of communications
- Simplify the complex
- Highlight customer benefits
- Reassure customers
- Connect with customers
- Have some fun

## Customer-first framing ("you" focus)
- Prefer **you** over **we** whenever possible.
- Avoid talking about Dodo unless it helps the customer understand what they need to do next.

**Example**
- **Avoid:** Welcome to Dodo, we’ve got your order, and we’re excited to have you onboard.
- **Use:** Welcome to Dodo, your order has been received. You’re now one step closer to unlocking real deal value with your internet plan.
